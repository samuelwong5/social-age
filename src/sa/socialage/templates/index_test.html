What is your social age?

Mark Twain famously said "most men die at 27, we just bury them at 72". He meant to imply that at 27 people stop living interesting, creative lives and settle down. Really what he was talking about was social age - which is defined as much by how people act as it is years since birth.


Inspired by this, we have collected data from social media users that self report age and used this to construct a model of social age based on what people follow. Sign in now with facebook or twitter to get an estimate of your social age!

Guess my social age - Imperial College year 3 group project

Guess my social age

With the advent of social media in the past decade, a person can often be judged by their activity on these websites by their peers or even their employers. From a survey conducted by an online career website, approximately half of the employers discard a job seeker's resume after checking their Facebook page. Social profile has become such an important " first impression" in the eyes of the public today These "social media actions" taken such as likes, tweets, and follows gradually builds up an individual entity over the internet. Likes and follows towards a certain interest, a belief and a certain celebrity form theses individuals into cluster of groups over the internet.

Our application aims to take a user's Facebook and Twitter data and predict a user's age as accurate as possible through a statistical model. The result of the prediction will hereafter be referred to as "social age". As the "social age" is based on a predictive algorithm that analyzes the user's public interests on social media (by means of 'liking' a page on Facebook or 'following' another user on Twitter), the result is often strongly correlated with the user's true age, which is in turn strong correlated with a person's maturity.

Moreover, the "social age" offers a new benchmarks in terms of the way individual can perceive themselves. In the Age of information, an individual social maturity may sometimes better describe the maturity perceived by the actual age. From the results, the social age forms cluster of group following the same interest, performing similar "social media actions". Such that individuals may discover new interest or likes base on other peers who shares the same social age. In extension to finding the "social age", the project means to demonstrates additional social information about an individual and their relation with their social peers that they may otherwise not know about themselves.
